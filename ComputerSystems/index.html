Computer Systems

These are my notes on the book Computer Systems: a Programmer's Perspective. I'm aiming to break down each chapter in a clear and concise way, focusing on what's relevant to a Cyber Security student.

Of course, the book carries much more volume in terms of detail, and there are certain topics I didn’t take notes on and was enough with simply reading through, so maybe you'd want to refer to the corresponding chapters for a more in-depth discussion.
